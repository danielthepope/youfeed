Privacy

YouFeed does not collect any data about you or your usage at all.

You're welcome.

All data processing takes place solely on your computer, and no cookies of any kind are stored or eaten.

However, this does mean that if anything goes wrong, I don't receive any automatic notification. Therefore, if you attempt to open a link that you think should work but doesn't, please get in touch making sure you include the broken link.

Help

Using the bookmarklet

I put an animated demo of how to use the bookmarklet on the YouFeed Tumblr page.

Or, the copy and paste method

If you're watching a video on YouTube, and you decide to subscribe to that user on feedly, first click onto the user's channel page.

Then copy the URL of the channel's page

Paste it into YouFeed then click the red button

The channel will open up in Feedly, which you can then subscribe to by clicking Follow.
